Nutty Cinnamon Bars

Description

These bars are quick and easy to make with a bake time of only 20 minutes! Furthermore, these bars are healthy, and no one would ever know. Another benefit of these bars are that the ingredients are easy to obtain and quite inexpensive.

Nutty cinnamon bars only require 5 easy steps and in a total of 55 minutes everyone can enjoy. Each batch holds a total of 18 bars, so nobody will go a whole batch with out enjoying at least one bar! This recipe should definitely be at the top of anyone's list.

Ingredients:

    Bars:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup low calorie natural sweetener
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened apple sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 cup chopped nuts

    • Icing:

  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar substitute
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 11x7-inch baking dish.
  2. Combine flour, sweetener, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl; mix until well combined.
  3. Whisk together egg, oil, applesauce, and honey in a second bowl until well combined. Stir into the dry ingredients. Fold in nuts. Transfer batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth out the surface.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 18 to 22 minutes. Remove from oven and allow to cool.
  5. For the icing, combine powdered sugar substitute, butter, honey, almond milk, vanilla, and cinnamon in a bowl. Spread or drizzle icing onto the cooled bars.
